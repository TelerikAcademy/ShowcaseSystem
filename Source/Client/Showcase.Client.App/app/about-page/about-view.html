About us

Telerik Academy is a leading tech-ed initiative, established by Telerik, a Progress Company in 2009, to address the shortage of skillful IT professionals in Bulgaria. By providing comprehensive trainings on software development and hands-on experience with the latest cutting-edge technologies, Telerik Academy helps people of all ages start a successful career in the IT sector. All courses at Telerik Academy are offered both onsite and online, for free.

Trainings

Telerik Software Academy

A 14-month comprehensive onsite training in software engineering for students and young professionals. It includes courses on C#, HTML, CSS, JavaScript, object-oriented programming, high-quality code, quality assurance as well as advanced software technologies. All courses are also offered online.

Telerik School Academy

A training program preparing students ages 13-19 for national and international contests in informatics.

Telerik Algo Academy

A training program preparing students ages 13-19 for national and international contests in informatics.

Telerik Kids Academy

A national educational initiative for teaching youngsters in Bulgaria, ages 10-12, how to code. Telerik Kids Academy curriculum consists of two learning tracks: algorithmic programming and game development.
